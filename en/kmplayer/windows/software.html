KMPlayer / KMP Plus is the most powerful media center of Korean developers. Its functionality is so rich that it will take more than one hour to list and describe them.

The player supports almost all currently used multimedia file formats including DVD, VCD, AVI, MKV, Ogg Theora, OGM, 3GP, MPEG-1/2/4, WMV, RealMedia and QuickTime. It has a built-in libavcodec library which already contains all sorts of filters and codecs for comfortable playback of almost any video and audio formats including streaming data.

The new "KMP+" is focused on satisfying the most diverse tastes and preferences of residents of different countries of the world. The choice offered to the user is really impressive – it includes videos of many formats, music of different genres, multimedia applications of various directions, a fashionable and modern "cloud service" for storing user information and ,of course, a variety of online games.

The KMPlayer / KMP Plus player has very wide and specific video settings, has high-quality support for subtitles of various formats, advanced video and audio capture capabilities are implemented, it can play under-downloaded or damaged AVI files, as well as play music files directly from archives and MUCH more.
