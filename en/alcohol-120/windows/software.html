Alcohol 120% is a powerful program for burning CD/DVD/Blu-ray discs, creating virtual drives on your computer, and emulating data from an optical disc. This application can write to a hard drive (hard drive, flash drive) an image of a disc with data, audio, or video information, and then provide access to this data to other programs as if the optical drive you are looking for is actually in the drive. This is especially true for many computer games which are run only from the disc.

In addition, saving the disc image as a specific archive on your computer is much safer than storing the data on regular CD/DVD/Blu-ray discs. That is why this program can also be considered as a backup application: in case an optical disc is damaged, you can always write a new one from the image to your PC.

Interestingly, here you can create up to 31 virtual drives and work with each of them simultaneously. In this way, the problem of replacing a standard optical drive is solved by the software method, where in principle the CD/DVD drive itself is needed only for the primary creation of the drive image.

If you still use various optical disks for work, study, or entertainment and need to copy/write/erase them regularly, Alcohol 120% will also help you with this task - perform all operations with disks as you see fit. There is also a function to check the quality of the recording.

The main features of the application:

  • Creation of up to 31 virtual drives (SCSI Pass Through Direct (SPTD) layer is used).
  • CD, DVD, and Blu-ray disc image creation, supporting MDF/MDS, CCD, BIN/CUE, ISO, CDI, BWT, B5T, B6T, BWI, BWS, BWA, ISZ formats.
  • Burn CD, DVD, and Blu-ray disc images.
  • Instantly copy from disc to disc.
  • Erase CD-RW, DVD-RW, and BD-RE discs.
  • Search for disc image files on your computer.
  • Exporting and importing image file lists.

It is worth paying your attention to the additional free version of the program called Alcohol 52%, which differs only in the lack of possibility to burn CD and DVD discs.
