MSI Afterburner is a functional utility from the company MSI, which is designed for overclocking NVIDIA and AMD video cards. With the help of the program, you can adjust the power supply voltage of the GPU and video memory, the core frequency, control the fan speed, as well as monitor the state of the graphics card in real time, such as its current temperature, clock frequency, voltage, etc.

It supports working with NVIDIA GeForce 8X00 and newer video cards, as well as AMD Radeon 3000 and newer. In addition, the user can test his video card for stability using the MSI Combustor tool, based on the engine of the famous FurMark benchmark.

The main features of MSI Afterburner:

  • Adjustment of the power supply voltage of video cards and the video memory chip.
  • Monitoring of switching between 2D and 3D modes.
  • The ability to control the cooling system of the video card.
  • The ability to create personal profiles (up to 5 pieces) with switching by pressing the "hotkey".
  • Monitoring FPS in 3D applications and displaying indicators in superimposed on-screen menus and the system tray.
  • Full support for 64-bit applications.
  • The ability to change the design themes.
  • The ability to record the best game moments and share them with friends.
