Skype is the Android version of the famous program for voice or video calls over the Internet and the exchange of text messages, files. This is much cheaper than regular international calls, and with a wireless Wi-Fi network, calls through this application become almost free. Wi-Fi, 3G/4G/5G and LTE/EDGE/GPRS networks are supported.

You will be able to call not only the numbers in the system itself, but also to ordinary mobile and landline phones at favorable rates. The program has the ability to create group video chats for up to 25 people.
