Google Chrome is the popular browser from Google which has finally reached one of the most popular mobile platforms today, providing users of smartphones and tablets based on Android with faster, more comfortable and safer work on the Internet.

Among the main features of the browser, it is possible to distinguish the presence of support for all modern web standards (HTML5, etc.), the ability to control gestures, automatic zooming of the pages viewed (helps not to miss the desired link), as well as synchronization of user settings and bookmarks between different devices. It should be noted that there is no Flash support.
