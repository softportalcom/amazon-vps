KMPlayer is a free and very easy-to-use tool for viewing video files in the highest quality with maximum control. KMPlayer has the ability to control subtitles, video playback speed, playlists and much more. In addition, the application supports HD videos and 30 languages.

Key Features:

  • A floating screen that allows you to continue watching videos when sending messages or browsing the web.
  • Support for all subtitle formats with full synchronization control.
  • The ability to slow down or speed up any video.
  • Support for gesture control.
  • Hardware acceleration support.
  • The ability to use an external codec.
  • Play videos stored in Google Drive cloud storage.
