WinRAR - it is difficult to imagine daily work with archives without this popular, very convenient and easy to use archiver.

Supports work with such archive formats as: RAR, ZIP, 7Z, ACE, ARJ, BZ2, CAB, GZ, JAR, LZH, TAR, UUE, Z, ISO providing maximum speed and data compression.

Among the rich functionality of WinRAR is the ability to repair damaged archives, create self-extracting and multi-volume archives, work with password protected and encrypted archives and the ability to archive almost an unlimited number of files.
