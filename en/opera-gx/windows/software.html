Opera GX is a special version of Opera browser, designed primarily for gamers, and offers users a number of unique features, such as control of CPU and memory consumption, integration with streaming service Twitch, etc.

Also, there is fast access to Discord, YouTube, and Reddit, customizable sound and animation effects, the ability to customize the appearance of the browser, and integration of messengers Telegram, WhatsApp, Facebook, and Vkontakte.

As in the standard version, there is a built-in VPN, ad blocker, video viewing in a pop-up window, and support for extensions (has its own extensions store, as well as support for Google Chrome extensions).

Key features:

  • Availability of unique features to get the most out of games and web browsing.
  • Ability to set a limit on the browser's use of RAM and CPU resources.
  • Integration with Twitch, so you can see your favorite channels, get notifications when someone starts a new stream, etc.
  • GX Corner gives you access to the latest gaming news, current online offers for gamers, and information on exciting releases.
  • Customizable sound and animation effects.
  • Wide range of browser settings.
  • Built-in messengers Facebook, Telegram, Vkontakte, and WhatsApp.
  • Watch videos in a pop-up window.
  • Ad blocker.
  • Built-in VPN.
  • Support for extensions.
