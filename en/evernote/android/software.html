Evernote is a program that allows you to quickly and conveniently remember everything you want to remember, and view your notes wherever the user is, from any computer or mobile phone. The service is provided through a standard web browser or through a special program (local client) Evernote, available for most desktop and mobile operating systems.

With the help of the application, the user of an Android smartphone can make notes and to-do lists, take photos and attach ads, price tags, schedules to them; save voice, printed and handwritten notes, if possible, with the records tied to geographical coordinates. All this information is synchronized with the user's servers and other devices and becomes available wherever it is needed.

The main features of Evernote:

  • a powerful tool for creating notes, task list, shopping list, photos, pictures, email, etc.;
  • automatic synchronization and access to notes from the Web, programs on the computer and from a mobile phone;
  • sorting notes by attributes and tags;
  • filters for quick search of notes, including name, date, tags, presence of a picture, etc.;
  • the ability to open public access to individual notebooks;
  • geotags of notes that allow, for example, to find notes "made within a radius of several kilometers".
