Firefox is a fast, smart and reliable Internet browser. It has standard features for working with tabs, additional modules and synchronization with a PC and other devices.

The application can "predict" your search queries and displays the results in your favorite search engines, there is quick access to Wikipedia, Twitter and Amazon. Private mode in the browser will provide maximum security and blocks some parts of sites that track user actions.

The browser supports most add-ons: ad blocking, password and download managers, online players, etc. The viewed information can be easily shared on Facebook, Twitter, WhatsApp, Skype and other popular applications.
