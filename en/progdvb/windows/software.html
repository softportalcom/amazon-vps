ProgDVB is an excellent tool for watching over 4000 channels of Internet TV, analog TV, satellite (DVB-S) and cable (DVB-S2, DVB-C) TV, IPTV, and listening to digital radio. In addition, with the help of the program you can search, view and record videos from YouTube, and there is also the ability to view files from the local drive and broadcast video/audio stream to the network.

Main features:

  • Support for most DVB, ISDB-T, and ATSC devices, including DiSEqC and CAM functions;
  • Simultaneous work with several boards;
  • Timeshift function;
  • Support HDTV including H.264/AVC;
  • Built-in equalizer;
  • Audio/video streaming recording to a file;
  • Availability of program guide (EPG, XmlTV, JTV);
  • The presence of Teletext and subtitles;
  • Broadcast channels over the network;
  • Built-in scheduler;
  • The presence of parental control;
  • Support for plugins;
  • The possibility of changing skins.
